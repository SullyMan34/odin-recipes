This is pizza. Pizza is good. How do you make pizza? EASY!


Ingredients:

  • 3.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 0.25 oz dry yeast
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups Mozzarella cheese
  • 4 tablespoons chopped basil

Steps:

  1. Mix warm water and yeast. Let sit until yeast foams/
  2. Add flour and knead, then let rise under a damp cloth
  3. Spread dough into circular shape, then layer tomato sauce (you can mix in seasonings in sauce before layering)
  4. Top with cheese and basil
  5. Grill on the grill (I'm tired of making lists right now, I want to go learn other things now)
